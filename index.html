Private Transition & Fulfillment Coaching

For expat spouses and partners navigating global lives

"I received a job offer but we need to move" — Starting a new life again...

Moving abroad to support a partner’s career can be exciting, but it also comes with unique personal and emotional challenges. Some of the most common challenges are:

  • Loss of identity: Putting careers on hold, lack of personal purpose
  • Isolation: Difficulty making meaningful connections
  • Culture shock: Navigating new norms and expectations
  • Emotional stress: Feelings of loneliness, anxiety, or depression
  • Lack of support: Leaving friends, family, and routines behind
  • Parenting challenges: Adapting children to a new system alone
  • Visa limitations: Not being able to work or gain independence
  • Relationship strain: Shifting dynamics in marriage or partnership
