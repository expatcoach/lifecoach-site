ME

Hi, I’m Gala! I’ve lived across multiple cultures and reinvented myself many times—each chapter adding to how I support others through transformation. I hold a Master of Science in Finance and am a transformation mentor, life coach, occasional writer, entrepreneur, passionate artist, and networker who has experienced the globally mobile life myself.

I’m in an intercultural, bilingual marriage and raising a trilingual cross-cultural child. Over the years, I’ve lived in Russia, Latvia, the UK, Italy, Spain, and Switzerland, navigating countless moves, transitions, and cultural shifts along the way. I currently live in London but work with clients all around the world, embracing location independence.

I know firsthand how exciting yet challenging life abroad can be. From adapting to new languages and cultures to building a sense of home and belonging far from family, the journey is filled with both opportunities and obstacles. Along the way, I’ve learned three new languages and now speak English, Italian, Russian, and Latvian.

Through my coaching, I support expats, global nomads, and internationally minded individuals to gain clarity, purpose, and confidence in their international lives. Together, we work on creating your own definition of “home” and designing the life you want to thrive in—wherever in the world you are.
