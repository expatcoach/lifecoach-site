YOU

You’re navigating a major life transition. Maybe it’s a new city, a new relationship, or even a version of yourself you no longer recognize. You may feel a sense of disconnection, uncertainty, or quiet unease—even when everything “should” feel fine.

This space is about you: your emotional world, your values, your stuck points, your voice, your vision. Our work together begins here—with where you are and where you want to be.

You don’t need to have it all figured out. You just need a safe place to begin.
